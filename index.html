Because there's no place quite like it. Kiwis build world-class applications and participate in the bleeding edge development of the global web. Let's celebrate our work!

The Badge

If you're a developer, designer, or some kind of kiwi involved in any way, show it off! Stick this badge onto your website or into your web app.

Not a coder?

The By A Kiwi initiative isn't restricted to coders and web apps only. If you feel like your field of work deserves recognition and celebration, tell us about it. New Zealand is great at many things, let's show 'em off!
